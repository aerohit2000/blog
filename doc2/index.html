    Professional Life

    I believe in earning mutual trust. Because I am product focused, I naturally enjoy reviewing and products that deliver good user experience. My goal is not to retire quickly but continue to work towards building or improving products forever, as long as a can.

    I am more of a visual person than a data person (That is why I take a lot of pictures or create graphics) I may not always talk about metrics or money straight away. This is because I think when you have a well designed product and a good team, than good metrics and money would start flowing in automatically.

    I believe that it is good product that brings in revenue - thats why i am focus on product first, struggles of end user, and not focus on profit margin all the time.

    I started my career with Thomson Reuters (Now Refinitiv) where I worked with Content, Market Data and with global Product teams.

    I am currently with Accenture for since 2014 working with amazing Tier-1 clients, gained experience in multiple roles, from operations management, to project management, financials to product managemenet support roles.
